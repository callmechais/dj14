Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 250ml milk
  • 3 ice cubes

Method:

  1. Peel the banana, then add it into a blender
  2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender
  3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds
  4. Your banana milkshake is now ready for serving!
